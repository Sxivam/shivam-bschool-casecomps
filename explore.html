FAQ

The honest answers.

The marquee tier is mostly curated to ~15–25 premier institutes (IIM A/B/C/L/K/I, XLRI, FMS, SPJIMR, MDI, JBIMS, NITIE/IIM Mumbai, IIFT, NMIMS, MICA, SIBM Pune, ISB, IMT, XIMB). But several are open to all MBA programs — Flipkart WiRED, Reliance TUP, EY Techathon, Hero Campus Challenge, L'Oréal Brandstorm. Each card shows its tier.
Most marquee comps offer a PPI to national finalists. A smaller set runs direct PPO routes — Bajaj Finserv ATOM, TAS InvicTAS, Tata Steel-a-thon, ITC Interrobang, IDFC FIRST FAME. Filter by "PPI" or "PPO" to see only those.
They're historical patterns, accurate to ±3–4 weeks. Use them to plan, then watch Unstop & the firm's careers microsite for the live link. Every one of the 85 entries was re-verified against live sources this season — but figures shift, so treat prizes as ranges.
Two months before launch. Early-June for Bajaj ATOM / Pidilite Bond / ABG HeadstaRt; mid-July for ABG Stratos / ITC / Amazon ACE / Flipkart WiRED / HUL LIME; late-August for Asian Paints CANVAS / Tata Steel-a-thon. Most are 1st-year flagships, so assemble teams right after induction.
