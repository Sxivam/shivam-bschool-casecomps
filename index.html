08 FAQ

The honest answers.

Mostly the marquee tier is curated to ~15–25 premier institutes — typically IIM A/B/C/L/K/I, XLRI, FMS, SPJIMR, MDI, JBIMS, NITIE/IIM Mumbai, IIFT, NMIMS, MICA, SIBM Pune, ISB, IMT Ghaziabad, XIMB. But several are open to all MBA programs in India — Flipkart WiRED, ICICI Beat the Curve, Reliance TUP, EY Techathon, Hero Campus Challenge, L'Oréal Brandstorm. Each card tells you which tier it sits in.
Most marquee comps offer a PPI (Pre-Placement Interview) to national finalists at minimum. A smaller set runs direct hiring funnels — Bajaj Finserv ATOM, TAS InvicTAS, Tata Steel-a-thon, ITC Interrobang, IDFC FIRST FAME carry explicit PPO routes; ABG Stratos, HUL LIME, Marico Over The Wall, J&J Imagivators feed PPI pipelines into the host firm. Filter by "PPI" or "PPO" to see only those.
The golden rule: two months before launch. So early-June for Bajaj Finserv ATOM / Pidilite Bond / ABG HeadstaRt; mid-July for ABG Stratos / ITC Interrobang / Amazon ACE / Flipkart WiRED / HUL LIME; late August for Asian Paints CANVAS / Tata Steel-a-thon. Most are 1st-year MBA flagships, so right after induction is the right time to assemble teams.
Roughly 60% of marquee comps prefer 1st-years (since the hiring funnel feeds summer internships). Examples: HUL LIME, ABG HeadstaRt, ITC Interrobang, Pidilite Bond, Dabur Verve, Amazon ACE, Tata Consumer Grow Beyond Better. The rest are 1st & 2nd year open — Marico Over The Wall, Reliance TUP, Flipkart WiRED, Bajaj Finserv ATOM, EY Techathon.
They're historical patterns — accurate to within ±3–4 weeks year-to-year. Don't anchor on them; use them to plan, then watch Unstop & the firm's careers microsite (LinkedIn careers pages also leak early). A competition like Reliance TUP has scaled its prize pool across seasons — treat figures as approximate ranges.
Quizzes (TATA Crucible, Reliance Quiz-a-thon) and pure hackathons are mostly excluded — they behave differently from case comps. Women-only flagships are included and tagged: Goldman Sachs Women's Possibilities Summit and J&J Women in Sales.
Built by Shivam Meet. The dataset is consolidated from official competition pages, Unstop listings, press releases, and verified winner reports — and re-verified each season. Cross-check live windows against Unstop & the firm's microsite before applying.
