The shape of the year

One heavy cluster. Aug through November is when the corporate calendar actually wakes up.

If you can only optimise one quarter as a 1st-year MBA, optimise Aug–Nov — that's when 30+ of the ~50 marquee comps launch, including HUL LIME, ITC Interrobang, ABG Stratos, Mahindra War Room, Reliance TUP & Amazon ACE.

Peak season

Aug → Nov

The HUL / ITC / ABG / Mahindra / Marico / Bajaj Finserv / Asian Paints / Tata Steel-a-thon / Reliance TUP / Amazon ACE / Flipkart WiRED window. ~30 marquee comps in 4 months.

Shoulder

Dec → Feb

Mondelez tail, GSK ECube, RPG Blizzard, HUL TechTonic finale, Vedanta XStrat, Tata Consumer Grow Beyond Better, then L'Oréal Brandstorm kicks off the global track in January.

The drought

Mar → Jul

Summer-internship season. Most flagships pause. Use it for L'Oréal Brandstorm finals (Apr), PwC Campus Contender mid-cycle, and prepping submissions for the August onset.

Things people quietly ask

The honest answers.

Mostly the marquee 40 are curated to ~15–25 premier institutes — typically IIM A/B/C/L/K/I, XLRI, FMS, SPJIMR, MDI, JBIMS, NITIE/IIM Mumbai, IIFT, NMIMS, MICA, SIBM Pune, ISB, IMT Ghaziabad, XIMB. But several are open to all MBA programs in India — Flipkart WiRED, Bajaj Finserv ATOM, ICICI Beat the Curve, Reliance TUP, EY Techathon, Hero Campus Challenge, L'Oréal Brandstorm. Each card tells you which tier it sits in.
Most marquee comps offer a PPI (Pre-Placement Interview) to national finalists at minimum. A smaller set runs direct hiring funnels — ABG Stratos, J&J Imagivators, Bajaj Finserv ATOM, ITC Interrobang, HUL LIME, TAS InvicTAS, Tata Steel-a-thon, Marico Over The Wall have explicit PPO pipelines for finalists. Filter by "PPI" or "PPO" to see only those.
The golden rule: two months before launch. So mid-June for HUL LIME / Pidilite Bond / ABG HeadstaRt; mid-July for ABG Stratos / Mahindra War Room / ITC / Amazon ACE / Flipkart WiRED; late August for ICICI Beat the Curve / Asian Paints CANVAS. Most are 1st-year MBA flagships, so right after induction is the right time to assemble teams.
Roughly 60% of marquee comps prefer 1st-years (since the hiring funnel feeds summer internships). Examples: HUL LIME, ABG HeadstaRt, ITC Interrobang, Pidilite Bond, Dabur Verve, Tata Consumer Grow Beyond Better. The rest are 1st & 2nd year open — Mahindra War Room, Marico Over The Wall, Reliance TUP, Amazon ACE, Flipkart WiRED, Bajaj Finserv ATOM.
